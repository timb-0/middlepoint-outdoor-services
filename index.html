Our Story

Built on Long Island.
Rooted in Craftsmanship.

MiddlePoint Outdoor Solutions was founded in 2009 by lifelong Long Islanders who believed that quality outdoor work shouldn't require a New York City price tag. Starting with two trucks and a crew of three in Hauppauge, we've grown into one of the most trusted names in residential and commercial landscaping across Nassau and Suffolk County.

Today, our team of 28 certified professionals handles everything from detailed garden bed installations in Garden City to large commercial snow removal contracts in Ronkonkoma — always with the same attention to detail that built our reputation.
